Reserve Your Fuel.


FastPump has partnered with over thousands of petrol stations all over India- Rural & Urban both. It uses Google Maps to show the available stations and their traffic. Reliance Petroleum ltd. , Hindustan Petroleum Corporation, Essar Oil Limited, Bharat Petroleum, Indian Oil Corporation and all the other major petrol station brands, have adopted FastPump.
Forget Fuel. Forget Fussy Cash.
Thanks to UPI, Credit & Debit cards from over hundred of banks, you can pay before you fill your tank. From the comfort of your home, pay cashless to your opted petrol station and receive a "Traffic alert" to come anytime you want.
There are many offers that you can avail, especially during the festive times. Make sure you have an account created with us and you regularly use our services.
Frequently Asked Questions:
  • What if there is an emergency? - You will be given a petrol can for storing fuel. However, to avoid the chances of corruption inspired practices, we will charge extra rupees.


  • How to use FastPump? - Create an account and provide the basic permissions so that our systems can find the perfect petrol station match for you. The program will allot you with a token. The token is unique and has numbers and letters of all sizes and shapes. After you have been allotted a token, you will be advised to go to a specific station between a time slot that expects lesser traffic.
